Privacy Policy for PenMouse S

Last updated: 08.12.2025

Thank you for choosing PenMouse S! Your privacy is extremely important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding it.

1. Usage Data and Crash Reporting

To improve the functionality and stability of the app, PenMouse S may collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This information can include details such as which features are used, screen interactions, device type, OS version, and technical error reports. All collected data is aggregated and anonymized, and cannot be used to personally identify you. These data are used solely to enhance app performance, detect and fix errors, and improve user experience. You can choose to disable this data collection at any time via the app's settings.

2. No Personal Data Collection

Aside from the anonymous usage data and crash reports described above, PenMouse S does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. No data is shared with third parties outside of the necessary services to support app functionality.

3. Offline Functionality

PenMouse S works offline. Usage data and crash reports may be transmitted only when the device reconnects to the internet.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. If we make any changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
